05 — What Could Go Wrong

Known risks and how we're handling them

RiskWhy It MattersHow We're Handling It
Code table has wrong dates Wrong code edition = inadmissible opinion Lane (CBO) verifies every entry against Clark County Dev Services records. Table is never AI-generated.
Your voice evolves but VOICE.md doesn't Draft quality degrades as language drifts Every filed report triggers a diff check. Version bump after each update. Sprint 2 produces v2.1.
Benchmark PDFs fail to load QA scoring runs against nothing — silent pass Sprint 1 adds explicit load verification. FAILED = immediate halt, no fake scores.
Complex cases produce unusable drafts Lane's review burden stays high for edge cases Sprint 4 creates two review tiers. Complex cases get every-stage review. Don't force single-pass on stair/ADA/multi-defendant.
Speed promise creates quality pressure Fast turnaround + bad draft = malpractice exposure No external promises until 5+ reports filed with metrics meeting targets. Production gate is Lane's call.
